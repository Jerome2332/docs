# Business in a Box

## Overview

The Business in a Box is a comprehensive business setup package that provides everything you need to start your property investment journey. This valuable resource is **included as part of our mentoring programs**.

<Note>
  The Business in a Box is automatically provided to all mentoring clients and contains essential business setup materials, templates, and resources.
</Note>

## What's Included

The Business in a Box contains:

- **Business Structure Templates** - Ready-to-use legal structures and documentation
- **Financial Planning Tools** - Spreadsheets and calculators for property analysis
- **Marketing Materials** - Professional templates for investor presentations
- **Legal Documentation** - Standard contracts and agreements
- **Process Workflows** - Step-by-step guides for common property transactions
- **Resource Library** - Curated collection of essential property investment resources
